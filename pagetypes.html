Modular Origami

This type of origami consists of many small individual pieces which are repeatedly connected to create bigger sculptures.

Pure Origami

Pure origami is made only from a single square sheet of paper. Cutting, gluing and taping is not allowed.

Kirigami

Kirigami is a version of origami where cutting is allowed.
