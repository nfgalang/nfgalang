The End?

This is where the truly brave and passionate origamists end up. Complex origami is similar to Simple origami but with added steps, planned folds and bases of paper to work on. Creating something considered complex is already an achievement but designing your own complex origami meets the criteria for Origami Master. Some complex origami has so many folds that instructions for it no longer exist, but a map of all the folds called a Crease Pattern is the only way to fold it.
